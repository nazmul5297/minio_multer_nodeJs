1. committee request will be 2 types. 
    a. adhoc/interim committee
    b. committee for election
2. minimum number of members required for a committee is 3 but no upper limit.
3. adhoc members can not be appointed to immediate committees if there is no 
   election committee in between but the rule is not applicable for government officers.
4. if the adhoc committee fails to do election then those members cannot be in the next interim/adhoc committee.
5. adhoc committee if approved will be transferred to main committee table.
6. if election committee, the election date will be active only.
7. committee request types short code:
    a. adhoc/interim committee: 'I'
    b. election committee: 'EC',
    c. elected committee: 'ED'
